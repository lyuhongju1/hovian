Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a tutoring session?

You can schedule sessions through our "Get Started" page or by contacting us directly. We'll match you with the perfect tutor based on your needs.

What are your rates?

Our rates vary depending on subject, tutor experience, and session type. Contact us for detailed pricing information tailored to your needs.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online tutoring sessions to accommodate your preferences and schedule.

What subjects do you cover?

We cover a wide range of subjects from core academics to specialized topics. Check our Subjects page for a complete list.

Can I request a specific tutor?

Absolutely! If you have a preference, let us know and we'll do our best to accommodate your request based on availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less notice may be subject to a fee. Contact us for details.
